About US

Yiwu Binyang Paper Co., Ltd


was established in 2014, which is located in the famous International Commodity Market City Yiwu, As a window of our factory in Yiwu City, We have established the good relationship with partners form the word.


PVC Binding Paper

Four production line for high quality PVC binding paper. We start the production line for producing the PVC coated paper since the year of 1986. Now we have 18 empolyee for producing different kind of PVC coated paper, with different embossing,color and craft. Such as Laini design, Single color, Mixed color, pearly shinning, Matel, etc. Good used for the certificate cover, note book cover, file holder cover, etc.


Textile Book Binding Cloth

Two production line for textile based paper. We start the production line for textile cover material since the year of 2012.Textile based paper with 6 empolyee for producing textile cover material.Products include the Ployster material, Cutton Material,etc. Good use for the Book Binding, Box Packing.


Fancy Paper

Two production line for Fancy Paper. We expend the Fancy Paper production line in the year of 2014. For producing the different kind of Craft paper.
